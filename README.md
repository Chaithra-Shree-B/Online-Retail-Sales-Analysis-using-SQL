🛒 Online Retail Sales Analysis using SQL

📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes an online retail dataset using SQL to uncover key business insights related to revenue, customers, products, and sales trends. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights for better decision-making.

🎯 Objectives
- Calculate total revenue generated
- Identify top-performing countries
- Find best-selling products
- Analyze customer behavior
- Evaluate average order value (AOV)
- Understand monthly sales trends

🗂 Dataset
- Name: Online Retail Dataset
- Type: Transactional Data
- Key Columns:
              - CustomerID
              - InvoiceDate
              - Description (Product)
              - Quantity
              - Unit Price
              - Country

🔍 Key Business Questions & Insights

💰 Total Revenue
- Total revenue generated: ~9.74 Million
- Indicates strong business performance at scale

🌍 Revenue by Country
- United Kingdom is the top contributor (~80%+ revenue)
- Shows high dependency on a single market

🏆 Top Products
- A few products contribute significantly to total revenue
- Demonstrates the Pareto Principle (80/20 rule)

👥 Customer Analysis
- ~4,300+ unique customers
- High revenue with moderate customer base → high customer value

💎 Top Customer
- A small segment of customers contributes a large share of revenue
- Highlights importance of high-value customers

📦 Average Order Value (AOV)
- AOV ≈ 270–280
- Customers tend to purchase multiple or higher-value items

📈 Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue shows fluctuations with peak periods
- Indicates seasonality in sales

🧠 Key Insights
- Revenue is highly concentrated in the UK market
- Few products and customers drive most of the revenue
- Customers exhibit strong purchasing behavior
- Sales are influenced by seasonal trends

🚀 Business Recommendations
- Expand into new markets to reduce dependency on the UK
- Focus marketing on top-performing products
- Retain high-value customers with loyalty programs
- Use seasonal trends for better inventory & campaign planning

🛠 Tools & Skills Used
- SQL (Aggregation, GROUP BY, ORDER BY, DISTINCT)
- Data Analysis
- Business Insight Generation
